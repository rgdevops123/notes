Q: What are the modes of vim?

   CMD, EDIT & EX mode


Q: In VIM, how do you change the shiftwidth setting?

:set shiftwidth=2


Q: In VIM, how do you shift 3 lines of code to the right?

3>>


Q: How do you delete all text above a certain line?

dgg

d is the deletion command, and gg is a movement command that says go to the top of the file, so when used together, it means delete from my current position to the top of the file.
